What is RedFinch?

Red Finch is a collective aiming to extend Perch CMS with quality custom plugins, apps and fieldtypes. Our releases are free, open source and developed with clients in mind, making it even easier make great websites that are powerful and simple to use.

Check out below for some of our latest releases.

Activity Monitor

The Activity Monitor provides a way of logging user actions when editing site content. It outputs a categorised list of actions based on the built in Perch Events system.

Requirements

  • Perch >= 2.8
  • PHP >= 5.4
  • MySQL >= 5.5
